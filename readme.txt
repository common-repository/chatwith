== Description ==

Chatwith lets you create a ChatGPT chatbot that is custom trained on your website and other data sources. Your chatbot can be embedded on your website to instantly answer your visitor questions about your products and services.

With this plugin, you can easily embed your Chatwith chatbot into your WordPress website.

== 3rd Party Services ==

This plugin uses the 3rd party service, Chatwith (https://chatwith.tools), to embed a chatbot on your site. The terms and conditions for Chatwith are available here: https://chatwith.tools/terms and the privacy policy: https://chatwith.tools/privacy.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I install my chatbot? =

Get the chatbot ID (found in the settings page for your chatbot on https://chatwith.tools/app/settings), and paste it in the textbox on the plugin configuration page.
